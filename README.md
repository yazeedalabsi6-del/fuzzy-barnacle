# Omar Al-Absi | عمر العبسي

One-page site for the law office of **Omar Al-Absi — Lawyer & Legal Consultant**, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. License No. **451716**.

Built with Next.js 14 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion.

## Features

- Bilingual EN / AR with full RTL layout and a single-click language toggle
- Parallax scroll backdrop and cinematic text reveals (Framer Motion)
- 3D tilt on service cards and the WhatsApp CTA
- Direct contact focus — WhatsApp is the sole call-to-action
- Palette: Rich Midnight `#020617` and Refined Gold `#C5A059`
